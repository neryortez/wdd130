Tegucigalpa, Honduras

Tegucigalpa is my favorite city because it combines mountain views, busy city life, and warm local culture in one place. The neighborhoods spread across hills, so many streets have beautiful overlooks, especially at sunrise and sunset.

I also enjoy the food, markets, and historic downtown areas where you can learn more about Honduras. Even with traffic and a fast pace, Tegucigalpa feels like home and has a strong community spirit.
